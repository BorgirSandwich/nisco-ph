About NISCO

A corporation built to modernize Philippine agriculture.

NISCO is an agriculture technology corporation focused on the Philippines. We develop digital tools and systems that help farmers, local government units, and communities work with better data, stronger coordination, and greater market access.

NISConnect

Our digital ecosystem, NISConnect, connects municipal governments, field enumerators, farmers, and markets on a single platform — reducing the fragmentation that has long held Philippine agriculture back.

Our Goal

Modern tools for the people who feed the nation.

When municipalities have better data, farmers get better support. Our goal is to help local governments move from reactive planning to proactive governance — and to give farmers the visibility, resources, and connections they deserve.
